About Neo Health and Wellness Center

Compassionate healthcare professionals dedicated to improving lives in Gaborone, Botswana

Who We Are

Neo Health and Wellness Center is a premier healthcare facility in Gaborone, Botswana, specializing in comprehensive wound care, ostomy care, and continence management services.

Our team of highly trained nurses and healthcare professionals is committed to providing personalized, evidence-based care to help our patients achieve optimal health and quality of life.

Founded in 2015, we have grown to become a trusted name in specialized nursing care, serving hundreds of patients throughout Botswana with compassion and clinical excellence.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, specialized nursing care that improves quality of life for our patients through evidence-based practice, innovative treatments, and compassionate service.
