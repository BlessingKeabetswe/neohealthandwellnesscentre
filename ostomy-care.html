Ostomy Care Services

Providing expert support and compassionate care for individuals with ostomies

At Neo Health and Wellness Center, our Ostomy Care Services are designed to support individuals who have undergone colostomy, ileostomy, or urostomy procedures. Our goal is to ensure you feel confident and informed in managing your ostomy, with personalized care in a safe and private environment.

Types of Ostomy Care We Offer

  • Colostomy Care: Assistance with stoma cleaning, bag changes, and skin care.
  • Ileostomy Care: Education on diet, fluid management, and pouching systems.
  • Urostomy Care: Support with urinary diversion systems and preventing infections.

What to Expect

We create customized care plans tailored to your specific type of ostomy and overall health needs.

Our team educates patients and caregivers on proper ostomy management, troubleshooting common issues, and maintaining hygiene.

We offer follow-up appointments to monitor your stoma site, adjust care plans, and address any concerns.

Get the Support You Deserve

Whether you are new to living with an ostomy or need continued support, we’re here for you.
