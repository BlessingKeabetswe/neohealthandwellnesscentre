Accessibility Statement

Our unwavering commitment to creating an inclusive digital experience for all patients and visitors

Our Commitment to Accessibility

At Neo Health and Wellness Clinic, we believe healthcare information should be accessible to everyone, regardless of ability or technology used.

We are dedicated to ensuring our digital platforms meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. Our team works diligently to create an online experience that accommodates individuals with visual, auditory, motor, cognitive, and other disabilities.

Our accessibility efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits by both automated tools and human evaluators
  • Ongoing staff training on accessibility best practices
  • Collaboration with disability advocacy groups
  • Implementation of user feedback to improve accessibility

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing journey, and we are committed to continuous improvement of our digital properties to serve all members of our community.

Accessibility Features

We've implemented numerous features to enhance accessibility for all users:

Keyboard Navigation

All functionality available by mouse is also available using only a keyboard, with visible focus indicators for interactive elements.

Visual Adjustments

Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality. Sufficient color contrast is maintained throughout.

Screen Reader Compatible

Proper semantic HTML and ARIA landmarks ensure compatibility with JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and other screen readers.

Multimedia Accessibility

Videos include captions and audio descriptions. Transcripts are provided for audio content.

Readable Content

We use clear, simple language and consistent navigation. Content is organized with proper headings and labels.

Responsive Design

Our website adapts to various screen sizes and orientations, maintaining accessibility across devices.

While we strive to make all areas of our website accessible, some third-party content or legacy documents may not fully meet accessibility standards. We are actively working to address these issues and welcome your feedback.

Technical Compliance

Our website has been designed and developed with accessibility in mind from the ground up. We adhere to the following standards and guidelines:

  • WCAG 2.1 Level AA: We meet or exceed the success criteria outlined in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1
  • Section 508: Compliance with U.S. federal accessibility requirements
  • ADA Title III: Alignment with Americans with Disabilities Act requirements
  • EN 301 549: Meeting European accessibility standards

We recommend using the latest versions of modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with updated assistive technologies for the best experience.

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge the following areas may have limitations:

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Third-party widgets or embedded content may have varying accessibility
  • Complex medical diagrams may have simplified text alternatives

We are actively working to improve these areas and welcome suggestions for enhancement.

We Value Your Feedback

Your experience matters to us. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please let us know. We treat all feedback with the highest priority.
